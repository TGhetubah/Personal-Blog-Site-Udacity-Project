ManChild BBQ was created out of an autistic child's love for grilling and BBQ. We want to share that love with you. Jacob was diagnosed with Autism at age 3 and a brain malformation at age 4. He underwent brain surgery at age 5, but the damage to his fine motor skill development had already been done. As you can see, however, it didn't make a dent on his physical growth. Jacob is our gentle giant. He loves cooking outside. He loves eating what he has cooked. And he LOVES meeting his "friends" when you recognize him in public. Please do not hesitate in speaking if you ever come across us "in the wild" because it truly brightens his day. Be sure to follow us on Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, and TikTok. In the meantime, "let's get this on the smoker!"
